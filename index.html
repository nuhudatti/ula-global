The 5IRE Story

How 5IRE brilliant minds changed IBBUL forever

It started with a simple observation: students were struggling to find past questions and notes. We were those students. Instead of complaining, we decided to build the solution ourselves.

Born from Frustration

Late nights before exams, no past questions, no notes. We knew there had to be a better way.

Built with Passion

300+ hours of coding. 50+ cups of coffee. 1 mission: Help every IBBUL student succeed.

Growing Together

From 5IRE students to serving thousands. Every download fuels our passion to do more.

Our Mission

To democratize education at IBBU by making every academic resource freely accessible to every student, regardless of department or level.
